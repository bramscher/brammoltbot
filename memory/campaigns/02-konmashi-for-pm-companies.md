# Campaign 2: Konmashi ‚Äî Marketing to Property Management Companies

## Overview
Promote Konmashi as the AI-powered social media solution for property management companies. Primary target: Appfolio users (70K+ customers). Position Konmashi as the automation layer that turns Appfolio data into social media content.

## Target Audience

### Primary: Property Management Company Owners/Marketers
- Demographics: 35-60, business owners or marketing managers
- Company size: 50-2000 doors under management
- Pain points:
  - No time for social media
  - Vacancies sitting too long
  - Inconsistent posting
  - Can't afford dedicated marketing staff
  - Know they *should* be on social but aren't

### Secondary: Appfolio Users Specifically
- Already invested in PM software
- Tech-forward, willing to adopt new tools
- Looking for integrations that enhance their stack
