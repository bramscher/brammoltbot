## Product Profiles

### Remy ‚Äî AI Meal Planning
**What it does:**
- Personalized meal plans based on dietary preferences, restrictions, health goals
- Grocery lists generated automatically
- Recipe suggestions with nutrition info
- AI adapts to user feedback and preferences over time

**Target audience:**
- Health-conscious individuals (25-55)
- Busy professionals who want to eat healthy but don't have time to plan
- People with dietary restrictions (keto, vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
- Weight loss / fitness goal seekers
- Families wanting organized meal planning

**Pain points solved:**
- "What's for dinner?" fatigue
- Decision paralysis around healthy eating
- Wasted food from unplanned grocery shopping
- Inconsistent nutrition

---

### MED ‚Äî Health & Wellness Podcast Summaries
**What it does:**
- Takes long-form health/wellness podcasts (Huberman Lab, etc.)
- Creates short audio summaries (5-10 min) with key takeaways
- "Blinkist for podcasts" concept
- Users get the insights without the 2-hour time commitment

**Target audience:**
- Busy professionals interested in health optimization
- Podcast lovers who don't have time for full episodes
- Biohackers, health enthusiasts, longevity-focused individuals
- Lifelong learners who want to stay informed

**Pain points solved:**
- "I want to learn but don't have 2 hours for a podcast"
- Information overload ‚Äî too many podcasts, can't keep up
- Missing key insights from trending health content
- Wanting the highlights without the filler
